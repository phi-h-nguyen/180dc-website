What We Do

The Rukmini Foundation

The Rukmini Foundation is a Pittsburgh-based non-profit organization that raises funding for the education of underprivileged girls in Nepal.

What We've Done:

  • Use Salesforce and Tableau to create standardized frameworks and storage structure for data flow and analysis
  • Write Privacy Policies for donators and beneficiaries
  • Create a social media plan to reach more donators online

Ruach Bike Club

Ruach Bike Club builds confidence and helps nurture problem-solving skills. The club members, which consist of elementary, middle, and high school students, learn bike science, health, mechanics, and how to disassemble and reassemble bikes.

What We've Done:

The 180DC team helped the founder Mike White by digitizing their application and waiver processes and created guides to improve their website and social media presence. We also connected them with on-campus organizations and corporate partners such as BirdBrain Technologies and Uber to further their mission. The 180DC team also won a $1000 prize in Impact CMU's annual showcase that supported Ruach Bike Club's STEM Summer Camp.

Angel's Place

Angel’s Place is a local nonprofit determined to help single parents by providing early childhood education, child care, and family support at no cost or significantly reduced rates for families in need. They work to ensure that every family within the community has access to resources in order to succeed.

What We've Done:

Our CMU 180 Degrees chapter has helped Angel’s Place reconstruct data and expand upon their current marketing plan. Previously, their donor data was stored across multiple platforms. Our consultants were able to convert this data from multiple Access files into DonorPerfect, enabling the staff to access previously locked data. Our marketing expansion is working towards increased awareness of the nonprofit as well as increasing the number volunteers and workers through google ads and other methods.

Boston Consulting Group

Boston Consulting Group, or BCG, is an American management consulting firm founded in 1963. The firm is one of the world's three largest strategy consulting firms by revenue.

As an organization, we visited their Pittsburgh office to learn more about their consulting techniques and gain their insight for our own projects.
