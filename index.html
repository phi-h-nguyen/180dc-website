Welcome.

Welcome to 180 Degrees Consulting at Carnegie Mellon! Whether you are a passionate student considering joining us, a potential client excited in what we offer, or a prospective partner interested in our talent, I want to thank you for visiting our site! 180 Degrees Consulting is a student-run consultancy for nonprofits and socially-minded companies, but especially at Carnegie Mellon, we are a young, growing organization with our people being our single most valuable asset.

We have talented students majoring in business, artificial intelligence, engineering, computer science, and more. Although we have different career aspirations, academic backgrounds, and diverse life experiences, we were all ultimately driven to 180 Degrees Consulting because of our unified passion for social impact.

180 Degrees Consulting at Carnegie Mellon was just established in 2019, but I am very proud to have seen the rapid growth we have been able to achieve in this short time frame from our impactful projects to our various corporate events.

I have been blessed to be able to bring 180 Degrees Consulting to the Carnegie Mellon community, and it has truly been one of the most valuable and rewarding experiences I have had during my time here. I am so excited to share our community with you, and we look forward to meeting you!

Sincerely,
Nadia Susanto, President
